About Me

I am a fifth-year Ph.D. student in the Department of Chemical Engineering at Stanford University, advised by Prof. Roseanna N. Zia. I am interested in computational studies and methods for colloid physics. I am supported by the NSF Graduate Research Fellowship. I expect to complete my Ph.D. in June 2022

In Fall 2022, I will be joining NVIDIA as a Deep Learning Library Software Engineer in the cuDNN team. I have previously interned at NVIDIA. I am interested in machine learning, deep learning, and high performance computing.
